Some facts about me

  • I think art is expression and knowing that is vital at all ages.
  • I used to pretend I was Martha Stewart and perform live arts/crafts demos for my stuffed animals.
  • I think education OF and ABOUT art is integral to building strong communities.
  • I love chocolate. And lattes. And most all things edible.
  • I want to be an arts programmer - which to me means someone who plays an important role in the designing, supporting,engaging of arts.
  • I’m a goal setter and list maker. I love to do 30 days challenges (health, fitness, creativity, writing, etc.)
  • I’m drawn to theatre because it is where I have found permission to be play and be childlike for life.
  • I love the way kids look at life honestly and without inhibitions. I learn a lot from them.
  • I love teams and community.
